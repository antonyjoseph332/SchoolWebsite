PRINCIPAL MESSAGE

St. Francis de sales school Jhajjar is a long cherished vision materialised by Delhi Fransalin Society. It has captured the imagination of Jhajjar and built for itself an enviable reputation before its inception. This achievement is due to clear cut vision, goal oriented efforts and ground level execution.

SFS School Jhajjar does not merely emphasise academics but a life of integrated growth of every child to prepare them lifelong achievers, keen and creative learners, progressive thinkers, cultural collaborators and above all compassionate and philanthropic human beings.

We have all along been guided by the saying of our founder Fr Peter Marie Mermier “The heart of our education is the education of the heart.” This is the spirit that moves us to work for the cause of Education. The maxim, “the quest for excellence is an endless path” is universally accepted. Numerous students will travel through the portals of our school to achieve such excellence. The end result should not be mere material accomplishment, but a life of love for our neighbours. SFS Jhajjar will remind them what Jesus taught his disciples “Whatever you do to the least of your brothers you do unto God.” St. Francis de Sales School Jhajjar is established to achieve this ideal. I express my deep gratitude to all those who collaborated to the success of SFS School Jhajjar.

ABOUT US

Fransalians are a body of priests and brothers of the Catholic Church, belonging to the congregation of missionaries of St. Francis De Sales founded by Fr.Peter Marie Mermier in 1838 at Annecy, France, for the renewal of Christian faith, to evangelise people and to educate the youth.
