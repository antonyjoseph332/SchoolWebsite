RULES FOR DISCIPLINE

  • Students must have their identity cards with them and regularly bring their school diary. The school uniform is to be worn on all days and for all school functions. Parents should see that their son/daughter does not wear the uniform outside the school when he/she is at home or elsewhere.
  • All the students must be in their seats before the teacher enters the class, during the absence of a teacher the monitor of the class, responsible for maintaining discipline, must be obeyed.
  • Students should take care of school property. They should not write or scratch on the desks, chairs, walls or doors of the school nor should they damage the property of others. Any damage done to school property must be remitted by the offender.
  • The school is not responsible if a student loses money or some article. Money should not be lent nor borrowed nor articles exchanged.
  • A student, who uses unfair means during tests/exams, tampers with the marks, answer sheets or report card will be given a zero in the subject.
  • The parents will have to give an undertaking after such incident. If the same student is caught for the second time, he or she may be asked to leave the school.
  • If any correction is to be made in the report card, it has to be brought to the attention of the school authorities within three days of receipt of the report.
  • Students who come to school with their domestic helpers should never leave the school premises before the person arrives. If the person is delayed, they should report to the School Office.
  • Students are not allowed to go to their friends’ homes for birthday parties, etc. directly from school.
  • Students are allowed to use the telephone kept at the reception, during C.T. period and lunch break on payment.
  • A student who misbehaves in the school bus will be debarred from using the same. Teachers and monitors are expected to maintain discipline.
  • Students must keep their cycles locked and in the cycle-stand they should not ride their cycle within the campus.
  • Students are forbidden to bring crackers, explosive and other dangerous materials to school.
  • Electronic gadgets like calculators, CD players, mobile phones, etc., should not be brought to school.
  • The use of polythene bags in the school premises is prohibited.
  • Students up to class VI are allowed to use only fountain pens.
  • Students from classes VII to XII are allowed to use gel pens.
  • Girl students should not wear ornaments. They are not allowed to use nail polish, kajal, mehendi, (If required to be used as part of a cultural event or festival It requires permission from the Principal in advance. For such permission one of the parents will have to meet the principal personally) make-up or grow long nails.
  • Boy’s hair should be neatly trimmed. Fancy hair-cuts are not allowed.
  • Girls’ with long hair should be in two plaits (upto Std. VIII) and in single plait. (Classes IX to XII)
  • Students involved in fights and those found bunking classes will be suspended.
